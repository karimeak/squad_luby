# Research Brief — IT Outsourcing Technology

**Language:** EN
**Date:** 2026-04-07

---

## Key Findings

### 1. IT Outsourcing Market Reaches $808B — On Track to $1.22T by 2030
- Confidence: HIGH
- IT services outsourcing market reached $807.91 billion in 2025, with 8.6% CAGR through 2030. Offshore software development alone: $178.6B in 2025 growing to $198.3B in 2026.
- Source: LitsLink Software Development Outsourcing Statistics 2026 — https://litslink.com/blog/software-development-outsourcing-statistics

### 2. 64% of North American Companies Prioritize Nearshore (LATAM) Over Asia
- Confidence: HIGH
- 58% of IT firms prefer nearshore partners. 90% of organizations considering new outsourcing destinations in 2026 are evaluating Latin America for proximity and skilled workforce.
- Source: Virtual Latinos Outsourcing Statistics 2026 — https://www.virtuallatinos.com/blog/outsourcing-statistics/

### 3. LATAM IT Outsourcing Market Reaches $18.45B
- Confidence: HIGH
- Fueled by competitive labor costs, skilled professionals, and time zone alignment with US companies.
- Source: Mismo Team Outsourcing Statistics — https://mismo.team/software-development-outsourcing-statistics-guide/

### 4. 75% of Companies Want Partners to Drive Transformation, Not Just Cost Savings
- Confidence: HIGH
- Business leaders prioritize specialized expertise and overcoming talent shortages over purely reducing expenses. Innovation-driven outsourcing is the new norm.
- Source: Auxis IT Outsourcing Trends 2026 — https://www.auxis.com/6-it-outsourcing-trends-impacting-2026-and-beyond/

### 5. AI Coding Assistants Used by 72% of Dev Companies — 35-45% Productivity Gains
- Confidence: MEDIUM
- Top performers report 20-45% productivity jumps with AI-driven delivery, reshaping how distributed teams operate.
- Source: SQ Magazine Outsourcing Statistics 2026 — https://sqmagazine.co.uk/software-development-outsourcing-statistics/
