## Research Gaps & Questions to Resolve

### Data Gaps
1. **Quantified ROI by company size:** What's the actual ROI of AI RevOps for SMBs vs. enterprise? Baseline metrics are missing.
2. **Regional benchmarks:** Limited public data on AI RevOps adoption rates in Brazil vs. US/Europe.
3. **Real productivity metrics:** Most AI vendor claims are self-reported; independent audits are rare.
4. **Churn prevention impact:** How much does AI-driven churn prediction actually improve retention? Need concrete case studies.
5. **Integration cost/complexity:** Hidden costs of integrating AI tools with legacy CRM stacks aren't well documented.

### Narrative Gaps
1. **Failure stories:** Most published content is positive. Need to find where AI RevOps projects have stalled or failed and why.
2. **Ethical & compliance considerations:** GDPR, data privacy, bias in AI forecasting—these aren't widely discussed for RevOps audiences.
3. **Change management:** How are sales teams adapting to AI tools? Resistance factors? Training requirements?
4. **Vendor consolidation trends:** Is the market consolidating (Salesforce buying), or fragmenting (best-of-breed)?

### Audience-Specific Gaps
1. **CMO perspective:** What RevOps AI features do marketing leaders actually care about? (pipeline visibility? lead quality? attribution?)
2. **CTO perspective:** What are the technical barriers to AI RevOps adoption? Data quality? API integrations?
3. **Brazilian market gaps:** Where is Brazil in the AI RevOps adoption curve? Are tools localized? Affordable?
