# Research Brief — AI Consulting for Enterprises

**Language:** EN
**Date:** 2026-04-07

---

## Key Findings

### 1. AI Consulting Market Exploding — $14B in 2026
- Confidence: HIGH
- The global AI consulting market is projected at USD 14.07 billion in 2026, growing to USD 116.63 billion by 2035 (CAGR 26.49%).
- Source: Business Research Insights — https://www.businessresearchinsights.com/market-reports/artificial-intelligence-ai-consulting-market-109569

### 2. Enterprise AI Adoption Hit 78% — But ROI Still Unclear for Most
- Confidence: HIGH
- AI adoption reached 78% of enterprises in 2025, delivering 26-55% productivity gains and $3.70 ROI per dollar invested. However, worker access to AI rose by 50%, yet many organizations struggle to move from pilot to production.
- Source: Deloitte State of AI in Enterprise 2026 — https://www.deloitte.com/us/en/what-we-do/capabilities/applied-artificial-intelligence/content/state-of-ai-in-the-enterprise.html

### 3. Finance Leads AI Consulting Services — 22.3% Market Share
- Confidence: HIGH
- Finance and banking lead the AI Consulting Services Market with 22.3% share in 2026, driven by fraud detection, risk management, and regulatory compliance use cases.
- Source: AI Consultation Statistics 2026 — https://colorwhistle.com/ai-consultation-statistics/

### 4. Skills Gap Is the #1 Barrier — 42% Cite Lack of Talent
- Confidence: HIGH
- 42% of organizations cited lack of skilled professionals and high implementation complexity as key challenges. Education (not role redesign) was the No. 1 talent strategy adjustment.
- Source: Netguru AI Adoption Statistics 2026 — https://www.netguru.com/blog/ai-adoption-statistics

### 5. 68% of Enterprises Use AI-Driven Analytics
- Confidence: MEDIUM
- Over 68% of enterprises adopted AI-driven analytics, enhancing decision-making efficiency and automation across multiple industry verticals.
- Source: Glorium Tech AI Statistics 2026 — https://gloriumtech.com/generative-ai-statistics-and-trends/
