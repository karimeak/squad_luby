**Flavor:** AI Consulting for Enterprises

## Best Angle

**"Your company adopted AI. It probably doesn't have an AI strategy."**

The contrarian adoption-vs-strategy gap. 78% of enterprises have adopted AI, yet 42% cite lack of skilled talent as their top barrier. This creates a powerful narrative for Maise's audience (CEOs, CFOs, VPs at fintech/tech companies): the problem isn't buying AI tools — it's having the strategic consulting to make them profitable. The $3.70 ROI per dollar stat gives CFOs a concrete number to anchor on, while the skills gap positions Maise as someone who sees the pattern others miss.

---

## Key Data Points

1. **78% enterprise AI adoption, but ROI still unclear for most** — Deloitte State of AI 2026. Massive adoption without matching strategic guidance.
2. **$3.70 ROI per dollar invested in AI** — Deloitte. Hard number that speaks directly to CFOs and finance leaders.
3. **42% of organizations cite lack of skilled professionals as the #1 barrier** — Netguru AI Adoption Statistics 2026. Validates the consulting gap.
