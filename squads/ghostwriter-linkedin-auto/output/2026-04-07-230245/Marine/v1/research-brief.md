# Research Brief — AI Agents

**Language:** EN
**Date:** 2026-04-07

---

## Key Findings

### 1. AI Agents Market Hits $7.6B — Growing 45.8% CAGR
- Confidence: HIGH
- The global AI agents market reached $7.6 billion in 2025 (up from $5.4B in 2024), expected to hit $47.1 billion by 2030 at 45.8% CAGR.
- Source: Grand View Research — https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/ai-agents-market-report

### 2. 85% of Enterprises Expected to Implement AI Agents by End of 2025
- Confidence: HIGH
- 57% already in production, 22% in pilot, 21% in pre-pilot as of August 2025.
- Source: Warmly AI Agents Statistics — https://www.warmly.ai/p/blog/ai-agents-statistics

### 3. AI Agents Automate 15-50% of Business Processes — But EBIT Impact Still Below 5%
- Confidence: HIGH
- Companies report 55% higher operational efficiency and 35% cost reductions. However, only 39% report any enterprise-level EBIT impact, and most say the contribution is still below 5%.
- Source: Appverticals AI Automation Statistics 2026 — https://www.appverticals.com/blog/ai-automation-statistics/

### 4. Customer Service Leads Automation — 30% Today, 50% by 2027
- Confidence: MEDIUM
- Customer service leads at 30% automation, expected to reach 50% by 2027. Some financial processes exceed 90%. By 2026, 40% of enterprise applications will include task-specific AI agents.
- Source: Master of Code AI Agent Statistics — https://masterofcode.com/blog/ai-agent-statistics

### 5. AI Agent Startups Raised $3.8B in 2024 — Nearly 3x Previous Year
- Confidence: HIGH
- Massive investor confidence in the sector, with venture funding tripling year-over-year.
- Source: Index.dev AI Agents Statistics — https://www.index.dev/blog/ai-agents-statistics
