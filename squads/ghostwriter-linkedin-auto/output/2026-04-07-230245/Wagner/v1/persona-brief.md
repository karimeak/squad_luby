1. **Compliance costs tripled:** US fintech compliance costs rose to $5-15M annually for mid-market players -- a 3-5x increase from 2020 levels (Source: SEC/OCC regulatory guidance, fintech industry surveys) [Confidence: HIGH]

2. **Consolidation is real:** Venture funding declined 40-50% from 2021 peak (~$238B to ~$110-120B annually), pushing profitability-first models and M&A (Source: CB Insights, McKinsey, Statista) [Confidence: HIGH]
